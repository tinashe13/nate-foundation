Foundation Units
  • Medical Unit (MU)

    The MU will focus on the special medical needs associated with FA including establishing a bone marrow transplant unit, which will assist in conducting affordable transplant, including training of medical personnel on associated procedures.

  • Knowledge and Resource Unit (KRU)

    There is limited knowledge in communities on FA, including the medical fraternity. The Foundation will establish a knowledge and resource centre with comfort rooms for families.

  • Counselling Unit (CU)

    The emotional and psychosocial toll for care givers and families affected by FA requires skilled counselling. The Foundation will, research on existing counseling services and gaps, develop a curriculum to train counselors, establish and resource a counseling training centre.
